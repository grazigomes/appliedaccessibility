  • The results of the on the WAVE website show 15 errors regarding the color contrast. Most of them are due to the low contrast between the color green on the background with the text colors with a 2.79 contrast ratio. The other contrast issue was in the header with the background color rgb(255, 255, 255) and the text color white as text.
  • To make the screenreader more readable the language "English" was added to the document .
  • The background color of main was changed to white and the color of the header text was changed to #003366 solving the problems of color contrast.
  • The table is more accessible because the headers were updated with mark up header cells <th> by setting the scope attribute to col or row.
  • The alt atribute was added to the images to make their screenreader accessible.
  • To make the audio accessible to hearing impaired people, I added a description of the audio under the file. To make the audio accesible to old browsers, a link was provided.
  • The tabindex="0" was added to the show/hide button to make keyboard-accessible.
  • To improve the semantics the <br><br> were removed and h1, h2 and h3 replaced the font size tabs. Paragraphs were added as well.
  • On CSS, the font selectors were replaced to h1, h2 and h3; the background color,text-shadow, and color for the header were replaced; the html background-color was replaced, and a header selector was added.
  • Labels were added to the forms to make them accessible.
  • As an extra improvement, a <caption> was added to the table.
